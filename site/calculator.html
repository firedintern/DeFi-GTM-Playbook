Incentive Durability

the finder · compare · the framework

The playbook's single most-repeated finding: incentives create value only when they unlock a loop that survives after rewards stop. The test is retention through utility, not acquisition through novelty. Describe your incentive plan and see whether it looks like a durable loop or mercenary TVL, and the one change most likely to move it.
