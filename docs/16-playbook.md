# Condensed GTM Playbook for a New DeFi Protocol

Distilled from the twelve cases, in rough sequence. Each step names its exemplars.

- **Wedge** ([Aave](protocols/aave.md), [Lido](protocols/lido.md), [Maple](protocols/maple-finance.md), [Ethena](protocols/ethena.md)): attack one balance-sheet pain point that becomes urgent during a market, chain, or regulatory transition.
- **Cold start** (trust: [Morpho](protocols/morpho.md); capital: [Spark](protocols/spark.md); sponsorship: [JustLend](protocols/justlend.md); credible liquidity: [Lido](protocols/lido.md)): inherit trust by building on incumbents, inherit capital from a parent balance sheet, or make early liquidity unmistakably credible.
- **Liquidity** ([Lido](protocols/lido.md)): spend incentives on the doubt-killer, usually exit liquidity for your receipt token.
- **Points, then token** ([Ethena](protocols/ethena.md), [ether.fi](protocols/etherfi.md), [Spark](protocols/spark.md)): run seasonal points targeting referrals, locking, and integrations; airdrop retroactively ([Uniswap](protocols/uniswap.md)); keep 50%+ of supply for long-duration user distribution. Judge the program on what remains when rewards fall, not on peak TVL (Maple's Drips retirement is the template for graduating out).
- **Composability loops** ([Ethena](protocols/ethena.md), [ether.fi](protocols/etherfi.md), [Maple](protocols/maple-finance.md)): get your asset into yield markets (Pendle-style) and as collateral into major money markets before scaling incentives.
- **Embed** ([Morpho](protocols/morpho.md), [Aave](protocols/aave.md), [Ethena](protocols/ethena.md)): pursue one flagship B2B2C integration (exchange, wallet, fintech) with revenue share. It derisks every later enterprise deal.
- **Institutionalize by removing workflow friction, not rebuilding the product** (Lido–Anchorage, Aave Horizon, [Ondo](protocols/ondo-finance.md), [Maple](protocols/maple-finance.md)): permissioned instances, custody integrations, governance exit rights, licensed entities. Treat custody stacks and compliance wrappers as distribution rails in their own right.
- **If early TVL is narrative-driven, plan the platform pivot before incentives decay** ([EigenCloud](protocols/eigencloud.md), [ether.fi](protocols/etherfi.md)): both pivoted from strength; waiting until churn shows up in the data is too late.
- **Re-platform from strength** ([Uniswap](protocols/uniswap.md), [Aave](protocols/aave.md)): once category-default, vertically integrate distribution (own chain, own app, own card) before the category commoditizes.
